Margarita

Description

The Margarita can be found in restaurants and beaches across America. It can be served on the rocks or as a slushie. You can find nearly as many flavors as you can imagine.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz blanco tequila
  • 1/2 oz orange liqueur
  • 1 oz lime juice, freshly squeezed
  • 1/2 oz agave syrup
  • lime wheel
  • kosher salt

Steps

  1. Add tequila, orange liqueur, lime juice and agave syrup to a cocktail shaker filled with ice, and shake until well-chilled.
  2. Strain into a rocks glass over fresh ice.
  3. Garnish with a lime wheel and kosher salt rim (optional).
